A time line of Lynden David Hall

  • 1974 - Born in Wandsworth, South London.
  • 1996 - Signed record deal with Cooltempo Records.
  • 1997 - First single, "Sexy Cinderella" and debut album, "Medicine 4 My Pain" are released. Hall is the first UK performer voted "Best Male Artist" by the readers of Britain's Blues & Soul magazine.
  • 1998 - Won the Mobo's best newcomer award.
  • 2003 - Appears in the film Love Actually and is diagnosed with Hodgkin's lymphoma.
  • 2005 - Releases third and final album, "In Between Jobs" and has stem cell transplant.
  • 2006 - Dies of complications resulting from the transplant.
